Our Story

Appalachian Bee Company was born from our love of honeybees and beekeeping on our farm in Eastern Kentucky. Having to drive an hour and a half to get beekeeping supplies was a real problem and we decided to become a solution.

Our love of beekeeping coupled with the need for a local supply shop with fair prices is why Appalachian Bee Company exists.

Come visit us on our farm in Prestonsburg or browse online. Thank you for supporting our family business!
